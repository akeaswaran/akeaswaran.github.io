Important Facts about Bipolar Disorder

Millions are affected, contrary to popular belief.

Approximately 2.6 percent of American adults have bipolar disorder.

It can appear in many different ways.

Bipolar disorder appears in one of four ways: Bipolar I, Bipolar II, Cyclothymia, Mixed Episodes, or Rapid-Cycling.
