Bipolar Disorder

Mania

Definition

excitement manifested by mental and physical hyperactivity, disorganization of behavior, and elevation of mood; specifically : the manic phase of bipolar disorder

Description

overly joyful or overexcited state

Characteristics

Mood Changes

• Switch to an overly happy or outgoing mood

• Extreme irritability

Behavior Changes

• Talking very fast

• Spontaenous and easily distracted

• Increase of interest and activity

• Restlessness

• Insomnia

• Having abnormally high self-confidence

• Acting impulsively and participating in high-risk behaviors
