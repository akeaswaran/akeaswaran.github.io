About Me

I'm Akshay Easwaran, and I'm a senior at Milton High School in Milton, Georgia, USA.

I'm very interested in the field of computer science and hope to major in it when I move on to college in a few years. I've been working with and on computers for most of my life and have developed a geniune fascination with how they work and how the software that controls them works.

I originally got into iOS programming when my family first bought an iMac. I followed a few basic tutorials from online and was immediately hooked. I eventually bought a MacBook Pro and started developing apps for my family members and school.

I started an iOS development club at my high school. With help from the club's members, I released both Milton GameDay and Milton ClassConnect for the students at my school

Last summer, I interned with CloudSherpas, a Salesforce.com partner in Atlanta, and assisted on a iOS development project that worked with the Salesforce iOS SDK and CoreData. Later this year, I hope to intern at a few more companies to gain more experience in the mobile development field.
